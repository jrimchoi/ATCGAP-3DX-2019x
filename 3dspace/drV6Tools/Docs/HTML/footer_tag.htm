footer Tag


Contents

The footer tag holds all the information for the footer of the page. This can include an image (defined in a footerImage tag) and text values (defined in footerText tags).

 

Example footer XML

<footer>
    <footerimage horizontalPosition="top" verticalPosition="left" scaleToFit="true" stretch="false" underlay="true" >
      <type>Document Template</type>
      <name>PDF-TEMP-0002</name>
      <revision>1</revision>
      <file-name>footer.jpg</file-name>
    </footerimage>
    <footertext style="small" horizontalPosition="left" verticalPosition="bottom" alignment="left" height="43" width="500">
      <text>Technical Information contained herein is subject to change without notice.  Some data may vary on machines.</text>
    </footertext>
    <footertext style="small" horizontalPosition="left" verticalPosition="bottom" alignment="left" height="16" width="500">
      <text>Part 1 - Application, Ratings and Performance</text>
    </footertext>
    <footertext style="small" horizontalPosition="left" verticalPosition="bottom" alignment="left" height="1" width="500">
      <text>Part 1 - Declarations of Power, Fuel and Lubricating Oil Consumptions, and Test Methods - Additional Requirements for Engines for General Use</text>
    </footertext>    
</footer>
