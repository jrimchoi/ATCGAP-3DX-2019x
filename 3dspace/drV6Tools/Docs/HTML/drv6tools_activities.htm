3dxtools Activities


Contents

3dxtools Activities are designed to offer a series of highly configurable utility modules that can be coupled together to perform complex actions typically required in an 3DEXPERIENCE ENOVIA implementation. It offers an alternative configuration-only approach to Enovia customisation that traditionally could only be handled by writing programming code.

This approach allows Design Rule to deliver and support a single tool set baseline across all our customers, which significantly reduces costs to our customers in delivering and supporting these essential additional ENOVIA capabilities.
