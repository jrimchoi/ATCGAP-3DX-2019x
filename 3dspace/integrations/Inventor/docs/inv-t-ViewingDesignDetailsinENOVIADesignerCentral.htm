Viewing Design Details in ENOVIA Designer Central

This task shows you how to view the CAD Portal view of a design in ENOVIA Designer Central from Inventor.

The ENOVIA > Explore in ENOVIA menu is available from the ENOVIA menu in Inventor or upon right-click in ENOVIA Open or ENOVIA Insert dialog box.

Before you begin:

The design must exist in ENOVIA database.

  1. Click ENOVIA > Explore in ENOVIA from Inventor toolbar if the design is active in Inventor to open the CAD portal view page.

    or

    Search for the design in ENOVIA Open or ENOVIA Insert dialog box. For details on searching for designs in ENOVIA from Inventor, see Searching Designs.

    Only one design must be selected in ENOVIA Open dialog box.

  2. Right-click the design and select Explore in ENOVIA.



    The CAD Portal View page of the design opens in the browser. For more details on using CAD Portal View page, see Using CAD Portal View section in ENOVIA Designer Central User's Guide.
