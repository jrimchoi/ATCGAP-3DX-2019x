Inserting Designs From ENOVIA in an Active Design

This task shows you how to insert designs from ENOVIA to a design that is the active open design in Inventor.

You can insert,

  • Part in Part.
  • Part in Assembly.
  • Assembly in Assembly.

You cannot insert,

  • Assembly in Part
  • Part in Part
  • Design in which you want to insert another design must be open and active in Inventor.

  1. Click ENOVIA > Insert from Inventor toolbar to open the ENOVIA Insert dialog box.

  2. Search for the design using Search option in ENOVIA Open dialog box. See Searching Designs.

    The search results are listed in ENOVIA Open dialog box.

  3. Click Insert or right-click the design and click Insert.

    The selected design is inserted to the design currently active in Inventor in the location (0,0,0).
