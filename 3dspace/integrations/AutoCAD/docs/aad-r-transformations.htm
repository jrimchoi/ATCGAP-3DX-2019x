Transformations Support

ENOVIA Collaborative Design for AutoCAD supports Transformations. The CSE checks for attribute mapping on the relationship "CADSubComponent". If the "relativeXform" attribute is mapped on the relationship then the Transformations functionality starts working.

During checkin the relative Transformations information is extracted and set on the corresponding relationship in the design structure. Similarly during checkout this information is received and set on the corresponding parent-child (assembly-component) relationship and the model is rebuilt.
