Inserting Designs From ENOVIA in an Active Design

This task shows you how to insert designs from ENOVIA to a design that is the active open design in AutoCAD.

Using Insert function, you can insert,


  • Part in Part
  • Part in Assembly
  • Assembly in Assembly
  • Part in Drawing
  • Assembly in Drawing

You cannot insert,


  • Assembly in Part
  • Drawing in Part
  • Drawing in Assembly
  • Part in Part

Before you begin: Design in which you want to insert another design must be open and active in AutoCAD.

  1. Click ENOVIA > Insert from AutoCAD toolbar.

    The ENOVIA Insert dialog box opens.

  2. Search for the design using Search option in ENOVIA Open dialog box. See Searching Designs.

    The search results are listed in ENOVIA Open dialog box.

  3. Click Insert or right-click the design and click Insert.

The selected design is inserted to the design currently active in AutoCAD in the location (0,0,0).
