Opening Designs From ENOVIA

Opening a design copies it from the ENOVIA database to your computer and opens or inserts it in another design open in AutoCAD depending on your choice. The following sections describe the concept of opening designs and the procedure to open designs, insert designs in another design active in AutoCAD and open another version of the design.
