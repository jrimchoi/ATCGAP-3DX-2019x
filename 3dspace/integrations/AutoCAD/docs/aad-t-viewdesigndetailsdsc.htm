Viewing Design Details in ENOVIA Designer Central

This task shows you how to view the CAD Portal view of a design in ENOVIA Designer Central from AutoCAD.

The ENOVIA > Explore in ENOVIA menu is available from AutoCAD or upon right-click in ENOVIA Open or ENOVIA Insert dialog box.

Before you begin: The design must exist in ENOVIA database.
Related Topics
Searching Designs
Viewing Properties

  1. Click ENOVIA > Explore in ENOVIA from AutoCAD toolbar if the design is active in AutoCAD or search for the design in ENOVIA Open or ENOVIA Insert dialog box. For details on searching for designs in ENOVIA from AutoCAD, see Searching Designs.

    Note: Only one design must be selected in ENOVIA Open dialog box.

  2. Right-click the design and select Explore in ENOVIA

The CAD Portal View page of the design opens in the browser.
