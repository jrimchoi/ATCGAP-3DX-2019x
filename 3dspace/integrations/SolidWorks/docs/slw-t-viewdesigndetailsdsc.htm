Viewing Design Details in ENOVIA Designer Central

You can view the CAD Portal view of a design in ENOVIA Designer Central.

Before you begin: The design must exist in the ENOVIA database.

  1. Do one of the following:

    • In SolidWorks, for an active design click Explore in ENOVIA on the ENOVIA CommandManager or in the toolbar of the ENOVIA task pane.
    • In the local vault view in Windows Explorer, select a file and click Display > Explore in ENOVIA.
    • In either SolidWorks or Windows Explorer, in the Check In, Check Out, or Get dialog boxes, click Explore in Enovia (toolbar).

  2. When prompted to log in to the server, type you Username and Password and click Login.

  3. If prompted, choose the appropriate security context from the drop-down list and click Done.

The CAD Portal View page of the design opens in the browser.
