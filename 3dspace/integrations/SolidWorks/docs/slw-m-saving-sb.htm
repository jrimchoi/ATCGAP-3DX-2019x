Adding Files to the ENOVIA Database

To add files to the ENOVIA database, you place them in a folder in the local view of the ENOVIA vault and them check them in. You can place the files in the local folder by saving them from SolidWorks to the folder, by copying and pasting the files, or by dragging and dropping the files.
