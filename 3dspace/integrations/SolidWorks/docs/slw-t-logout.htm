Disconnecting from ENOVIA

You disconnect from ENOVIA Collaborative Design for SolidWorks using a menu accessed from the ENOVIA icon in the notification area of the task bar.

Related Topics:
ENOVIA Task Bar Menu

  1. In the notification area in the far right of the task bar, double-click the ENOVIA icon to display the ENOVIA menu.

  2. In the menu, click Exit.

The connection to ENOVIA Collaborative Design for SolidWorks is disconnected and the ENOVIA icon is removed from the task bar.
