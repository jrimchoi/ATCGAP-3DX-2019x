Navigation Pane

The navigation pane on the left lets you browse vaults and folders.

The ENOVIA vault icon identifies the vaults to which you have access.

The first time you click a vault during your session, you may be prompted to log in. See

When you expand a vault, the navigation pane displays the workspaces and folders it contains.

You can right-click workspaces or folders to view their properties or perform folder operations.

Click a workspace to open the workspace view in the right pane.

Click a folder to open the file view in the right pane.
